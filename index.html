Vocal warmups

"MUM" warmup excersise

We use the "M" consonant and "UH" vowel for a light yet supported sound. Voicecracks are totally fine, just make sure not to force this excerise

"NAY" warmup excersise

We use the "N" consonant and "AY" vowel for a light sound that allows easy transition into the falsetto range. Voicecracks are totally fine, just make sure not to force this excerise

"HUNG" placement excersise

The "NG" sound is a great safe way to explore different placements for resonace. This helps developing higher screaming ranges.
